About

This is the rna base-pair steps database.

News


Thursday - February 24 2011 
The RNASTEPS database is in active development. 
This is version 0.1.
Please make comments and suggestions by e-mail to the author and maintainer of 
the website whose e-mail address is at the bottom of all pages.

Wednesday - May 11 2011 
Searching by prefered groups of data example.
A prefered group, say, GC.GC, can be displayed in a 
separate table.
This works as a practical example before adding full search capability of
Leontis-Westhof patterns and PDB and NDB id's.

Tuesday - May 17 2011 
Configuration and update of the full data for the 91 unique base-pair steps formed
from the seven predominant base-pairs in available RNA X-Ray data from the PDB/NDB.
The data are rendered in the Info page.

Also tutorial documentation on how to set-up a the django web-framework
is made available at:
django.pdf

Tuesday - May 24 2011 
A new search box has been added and the organization of the site is
being updated.
