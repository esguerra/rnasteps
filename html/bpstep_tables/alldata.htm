In the following image an RNA base-pair step is depicted using the Leontis-Westhof annotation convention for base-pairs. A circle denotes a Watson-Crick edge, a square denotes a Hoogsteen edge, and a triangle denotes a Sugar edge. Open polygons mean that the glycosidic bonds are in the trans orientation and filled polygons stand for the cis orientation.
